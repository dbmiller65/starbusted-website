About Starbusted

Starbusted is a community-driven project that aims to reduce littering by exposing coffee cup litterbugs. When you leave your Starbucks cup behind, your name is right there on it - and now, so is your photo on our website.

Founded in 2025, we believe that a little public accountability can go a long way in keeping our streets, parks, and public spaces clean. Join our mission by submitting photos of abandoned Starbucks cups with names clearly visible.
